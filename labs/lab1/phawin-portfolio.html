Exculpation reflections

November 13, 2024

In "Interactive Programming" course, I had been learning alot regarding game development and teamwork as a whole. Since not only it was the first time I develop a 3D game. It was also the first time I develop First-person game, and also the firt time I work with my college.

I had encounter many problem that I did not anticipated before, such as dashing into uneven terrrain can launch player into the sky, or enemy animation bug. I also did not anticipated that in the persepective of a tester the game is down right confusing. And lastly there are a lot of ideas that are not into fruition due to my own procrastination and inexperience, but it was an eye opening project for me nontheless.
